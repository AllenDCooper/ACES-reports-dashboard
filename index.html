Initial Reports

Select a report type:

The ACES Initial Class Report compares your class of students to nationwide norming tables. The "Student" menu below shows the results of all students who took the ACES Inventory (Initial Report).

  • Use the "Student" menu to view individual student results.
  • Use the "Order by" menu to sort the results from highest percentiles to lowest or lowest to highest.
  • Click scale names to view students who fall into low, moderate, and high percentile ranks for that scale.
HighModerateLow

The ACES Initial Roster Report shows your class's performance per scale. Expressed as percentile ranks, the bars indicate where each student compares to the national average in terms of low (1-25%), moderate (26-75%), and high (76-99%) percentile rank.

  • Use the "Scale" menu to review results by individual scale.
  • Use the "Order by" menu to sort the results by highest percentiles to lowest or lowest to highest.
  • Click on student names to view individual performance reports.
HighModerateLow

Select a report type:

The ACES Comparison Class Report compares your class's results on the ACES (Initial) and ACES (Progress) self-assessments for all scales. For each topic, the Initial score appears above the Progress score. Only students who have completed both the Initial and the Progress instances of ACES appear in this report.

Change Reports

Select a report type:

The ACES Change Class Report shows your class's average percent change in raw score, from the ACES (Initial) to the ACES (Progress) self-assessment, on all scales. A positive change appears green, while a negative change appears red. Only students who have completed both the Initial and the Progress instances of ACES appear in this report.

  • Use the "Student" menu to view individual student performance reports.
  • Use the "Order by" menu to sort scales from highest percent change ranks to lowest or from lowest to highest.

The ACES Change Roster Report shows each student’s percent change in raw score, from the ACES (Initial) to the ACES (Progress) self-assessment, on a particular ACES scale. A positive change appears green, while a negative change appears red. Only students who have completed both the Initial and the Progress instances of ACES appear in this report.

  • Use the "Scale" menu to review results by individual scale.
  • Use the "Order by" menu to sort the results by highest percent change ranks to lowest or lowest to highest.
  • Click on student names to view individual performance reports.

The ACES Change Institutional Report shows your institution's average percent change in raw score, from the ACES (Initial) to ACES (Progress) self-assessment, for each scale. A positive change appears green, while a negative change appears red. Only students who have completed both the Initial and the Progress instances of ACES appear in this report.
